ABOUT US

Gupta Enterprises is a Chennai (Tamil Nadu) based company that was set up in the year 1974. The company is running very smoothly with the acute business sense of its Partners- Mr. M. M. Gupta and Mr. M. Kishore Gupta. Procurement, processing, expertise and stewardship of our Managing Partner Mr. M. M. Gupta is our strength. He is the pioneer revolutionary in the Human Hair Industry.

Awards & Achievements

M/s. Gupta Enterprises, the largest and the Top Export Award Winners for Indian Human Hair from 1987 to 2011. Government of India recognized us as an export house, exporting top quality Indian Human Hair for over 39 years to over 33 countries in the world.
